Flix Critique Privacy Policy

Flix Critique is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information.

What information do we collect?

We collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact information: This includes your name, email address, and physical address.
  • Device information: This includes your IP address, device type, and operating system.
  • Usage information: This includes information about the pages you visit on our website, the movies you watch, and the reviews you write.
  • Demographics: This includes information about your age, gender, and location.

How do we use your information?

We use your information to:

  • Provide you with the Flix Critique service.
  • Send you email notifications about new movies, reviews, and other updates.
  • Customize your experience on Flix Critique.
  • Target advertising to you.
  • Conduct research and analysis.
  • Comply with legal requirements.

How do we share your information?

We may share your information with:

  • Our third-party service providers who help us operate Flix Critique.
  • Advertisers who want to target their ads to you.
  • Law enforcement agencies if required by law.

How do we protect your information?

We take security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Using encryption to protect your personal information when it is transmitted over the internet.
  • Storing your personal information on secure servers.
  • Limiting access to your personal information to authorized employees.

Your rights

You have the following rights with respect to your personal information:

  • The right to access your personal information.
  • The right to correct your personal information.
  • The right to delete your personal information.
  • The right to object to the processing of your personal information.
  • The right to withdraw your consent to the processing of your personal information.
